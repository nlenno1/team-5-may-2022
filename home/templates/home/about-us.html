What is Reach Out?

Reach Out aims to help anyone currently facing difficulties to take the first steps in reaching out to their friends and family for support.

Designed with the hope of giving confidence to anyone who is struggling with approaching a friend or a loved one to broach difficult topics of conversation, Reach Out is an App where users can send a message to someone they know, inviting them to talk.
